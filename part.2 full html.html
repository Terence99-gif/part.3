Good for People is dedicated to fostering positive change by empowering communities worldwide. Through our various initiatives, we strive to meet essential needs and create sustainable opportunities for growth, ensuring a brighter future for all. Join us in making a lasting impact.

About Us

Our History

Founded in 2010, Good for People began as a small community initiative in South Africa. What started with weekly food drives has grown into an international organization operating in 15 countries, serving over 1.2 million beneficiaries annually.

Vision & Mission

Vision:

"A world where every individual has access to basic needs and opportunities for growth"

Mission:

"To empower communities through sustainable development programs and emergency relief initiatives"

Our Services

Our Project Budget Allocation

Program Expenses (70%)

Direct costs for food distribution, educational supplies, medical aid, and community development projects. This is where the majority of your support goes.

Operational Costs (15%)

Includes administration, staff salaries (minimal), office supplies, and utilities essential for smooth functioning.

Fundraising & Outreach (10%)

Costs associated with raising awareness, volunteer recruitment, and securing further support for our initiatives.

Contingency & Reserves (5%)

Allocated for unforeseen emergencies, new project opportunities, and ensuring long-term sustainability.

We are committed to transparency and efficient use of funds. View our Annual Reports for more detail.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the mission of Good for People?

►

Our mission is to empower communities through sustainable development programs and emergency relief initiatives, striving for a world where every individual has access to basic needs and opportunities for growth.

Learn more about our core values and long-term vision on our About Us page.

How can I volunteer?

►

Becoming a volunteer is simple! You can start by filling out our Inquiry Form, specifying your interest in volunteering. Our dedicated volunteer coordinator will then connect with you to discuss available opportunities, required training, and how you can best contribute to our impactful projects. We welcome diverse skills and backgrounds!

What kind of impact do you make?

►

We are proud of the tangible impact we've made! Annually, we distribute millions of meals, engage thousands of dedicated volunteers, and serve numerous communities across multiple countries. Our programs focus on critical areas like food distribution, educational support, healthcare initiatives, and sustainable community development. Visit our Our Impact section for a visual representation of our achievements!

Are my donations tax-deductible?

►

Yes, Good for People is a registered non-profit organization, and all donations are tax-deductible to the fullest extent of the law. Upon successful processing of your contribution, you will receive an official tax receipt. Your generosity directly fuels our mission and helps us continue our vital work.
